About

Meet Sharijah Metamin, the master of her work.

Started with her love of baking home-made desserts like cupcakes to display her love and devotion to her family members. Her desire to master her baking expertise and art to venture the fondant cake circa 2011. She has since mastered her craft of creating edible arts depending on the requests of her clients.
